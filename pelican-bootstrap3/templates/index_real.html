About Me

I am a data scientist at Distil Networks. I previously worked as a data scientist for Qadium, Inc, data engineer at Parse.ly and a data scientist at RTI International.

I have a masters in operations research from North Carolina State University where I worked with Brian Denton on reinforcement learning for healthcare scheduling. I also spent a year as a grad student in math at the University of Virginia. My undergraduate degree from Grove City College is in mathematics; I also earned minors in applied physics and computer science.

In my free time, I enjoy conversation, reading, hiking, taking pictures, (more) programming, and good food.
